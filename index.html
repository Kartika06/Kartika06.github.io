"Black Swan"
is a song recorded by the Korean boy band, BTS. It was released on January 17, 2020 by Big Hit Entertainment, as the first single from their fourth Korean-language studio album Map of the Soul: 7 (2020).

"Wings"
is the second album by the South Korean boy group BTS. It was released on October 10, 2016 and consists of 15 tracks with "Blood Sweat & Tears" as the first single, and seven solo tracks recorded by each member.

"Love Yourself"
is the third Korean-language studio album by South Korean boy band BTS. It was released on May 18, 2018 by Big Hit Entertainment. This album is the group's third full Korean album, following 2016 album Wings.
